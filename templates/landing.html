LinkPath

Your URL-path-aware link & note aggregator

Prepend links.floholz.dev/ to any URL path to get a personal page for storing links and notes, organized by the path hierarchy.

Get Started Sign In
🔗

Path-Organized

Your links and notes live at paths. /github.com/user/repo keeps all your notes about that repo in one place.

📄

Links & Notes

Save links with titles, or write Markdown notes. Everything is rendered beautifully in context.

🕐

Hierarchical

Browse ancestor paths to see related content. The sidebar shows all descendant paths at a glance.

🔒

Private

All your links and notes are private. Only you can see them after you log in.

How it works

links.floholz.dev/github.com All GitHub links
links.floholz.dev/github.com/myorg Your org's repos
links.floholz.dev/github.com/myorg/project One specific project
